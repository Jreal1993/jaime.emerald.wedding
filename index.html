The wedding will be taking place in Ketchikan, AK so for those who've never been there, here's some things you might want to know.

For starters, when you arrive, you will need to take a ferry from the airport. This ferry runs every 30 minutes and is free for kids under the age of 5, $3 for kids 6-11, and $6 for anyone older.

During this time of the year, the weather will be somewhere in the 60's, and while it is unlikely, rain is always a possibility in South East AK, so make sure you pack a jacket!

For those planning to stay for a while, there are a ton of nice hikes, beaches to walk through, and tourist activities.

If you would like to fish, all you need to do is get a fishing license. You can either get one at the local Walmart or online.
